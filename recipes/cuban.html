Cuban Sandwich

Home

Description

The Cuban sandwich, also know as the Cubano, apparently originated in Cuban communities in Florida. Somehow, this sandwich made its way to Superior, Wisconsin, where I enjoyed one of the best sandwiches of my life. Anyways, give it a try.

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf Cuban Bread
  • 1/4 cup Mayo
  • 1/4 cup Mustard
  • Dash of Cayenne
  • 8 slices of Swiss Cheese
  • 6 thin slices of Deli Ham
  • 1 1/2 cups of Pulled Pork, heated
  • 1 large pickle, sliced thinly lengthwise
  • Butter

Directions

  1. Mix the mayo, mustard, and cayenne together to make the sauce.
  2. Cut the loaf in half, lengthwise, to get the top and bottom piece. Then, cut this loaf in half down the middle.
  3. Spread the sauce on both sides of the sandwich. Then, take the two bottom pieces, and put on each:
    • 2 slices of Swiss Cheese
    • 3 slices of ham
    • Pulled Pork
    • Pickle slices
    • 2 more slices of Swiss Cheese
  4. Melt butter on a pan over medium height. Grill both sides of the sub, making sure to press down with a large spatula, which will make the sandwich easier to eat. Remove when done and then enjoy!
