Chicken Alfredo

Home

Description

This dish is based on the classic Alfredo sauce, which, if you're somehow unfamiliar with it, is a combination of cream, butter, salt, parmesan, and garlic. It is typically served with a wider noodle, like fettucine, and is popular paired with grilled chicken. Here, we will focus on the base dish, and leave the chicken recipe to others.

Ingredients

  • 2lb Fettucine pasta
  • Butter 1 cup
  • Heavy Cream 1 and 1/2 cups
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Garlic 4 cloves
  • Romano 3/4 cup
  • Parmesan 1/2 cup
  • Parsley for garnish

Directions

  1. Cook fettucine for 8-10 minutes, then drain.
  2. Fill pan with cream and melt butter into over low heat. Add garlic cloves, and then salt and pepper to taste
  3. Increase heat to medium, and melt in both cheeses. Cook until sauce is thick.
  4. Make sure to taste sauce, add salt, pepper, or garlic as needed. If not thick enough, add pasta water.
  5. Plate up the pasta, and pour the sauce over. Garnish with parsley, salt, pepper, and parmesan flakes. Serve and enjoy!
